# AirPure Innovations: AQI Analytics Capstone Project

## Author

Shahnaz Bano

## Project Overview

AirPure Innovations is a Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Capstone Project focused on Air Quality Index (AQI) monitoring, pollution analysis, health impact assessment, and market intelligence.

The project demonstrates how Python analytics can transform environmental datasets into actionable business insights for a conceptual environmental technology startup.

## Problem Statement

Air pollution is one of India's most significant environmental and public health challenges. Organizations require data-driven insights to understand pollution trends, identify high-risk regions, evaluate market opportunities, and support environmental decision-making.

## Project Objectives

* Analyze AQI and pollution datasets
* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
* Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Classify AQI severity levels
* Identify pollution trends and seasonal patterns
* Evaluate health and business impacts
* Generate strategic recommendations
